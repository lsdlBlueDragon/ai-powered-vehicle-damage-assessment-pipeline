# Privacy Checklist

Before pushing changes to GitHub, check that the repository does not include:

- CarDD dataset archives or extracted images.
- Training outputs, checkpoints, exported models, or experiment logs.
- Google Drive share links that expose private files.
- Personal names, email addresses, phone numbers, student IDs, tokens, or passwords.
- Machine-specific absolute paths.
- Filled dataset license forms.

Large files should stay in Google Drive under `CarDD_YOLO11/`.
